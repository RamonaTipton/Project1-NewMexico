Albuquerque


Albuquerque is New Mexico’s largest city and has not only a vibrant nightlife, but also is full of historic and fun things to do with family and friends. When tired of the city life, travelling less than an hour will get you back to nature in the Sandia Mountains to east or do the Rio Grande bosque which runs through the middle of the city.


THINGS TO DO

Sandia Tramway, Old Town, Albuquerque Zoo, aquarium and botanical gardens and check out an Isotopes game.
